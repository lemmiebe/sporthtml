What is SCA?

  • Sports Challenge America is an innovative facility that brings together the most popular sports in one place!
  • We offer fun and unique experiences for sports lovers of all ages and levels.
  • Guests 18+ can participate in our many challenges to win amazing cash prizes.
  • We also hold tournaments where you can show your skills and be crowned a champ.
  • Want to host a birthday party, corporate function or other event? Sports Challenge America is the ideal spot for a gathering unlike any other. Just visit our Reservation page for information.
