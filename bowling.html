CHALLENGES

Have you ever flirted with that perfect game or felt like all you could throw were strikes? Bowl three in a row and your skills will be rewarded. State-of-the-art lanes in the area's newest and most unique multi-sport venue will offer you an opportunity to show off in front of friends and family.

A multitude of contests will be offered for everyone in the family. Compete to be the highest scorer, throw the most consecutive strikes or make that infamous 7-10 split. Bowlers will be delighted by the opportunities in a variety of innovative tournaments.
