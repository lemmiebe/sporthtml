  • Is SCA for all ages?

    Skill challenges are for ages 18 and older. There will be tournaments for all ages in a variety of different sports. Anyone under the age of 18 must have a waiver signed by a parent or legal guardian to compete in a tournament. Facility rentals are perfect for birthday parties, bachelor parties, corporate outings, or family events.

  • Do you have a driving range?

    No, we have a 130-yard par 3 hole for skill challenges and for putting and closest to the pin tournaments.

  • Is this sports wagering?

    No, sports wagering is presently illegal in the State of Florida. We offer individual skill-based sports challenges and tournaments in a variety of specific sports designed to test your abilities against other competitors.

  • Do you serve food and alcohol?

    Yes, if you are 21 or over we offer a wide variety of mixed drinks, beers, and wine. We have an assortment of delicious traditional American fare including burgers, fries, and grilled cheese sandwiches.
