Privacy Policy

Information Collection

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you express an interest in obtaining information about us or regarding our products and services as you interact with our website, reservations and/or transaction systems.

The personal information we collect may include names, phone numbers, email addresses, mailing addresses and preferences that you indicate.

Use of Personal Data

We may use the information collected from you to contact you by email, telephone, SMS, or other equivalent forms of electronic communication, such as a mobile application's push notifications regarding updates or informative communications related to the functionalities, products or services we provide.

We may also provide you with news, special offers and general information about other goods, services and events which we offer that are similar to those that you have already purchased or enquired about unless you have opted not to receive such information.

Payments

For electronic payments we use third-party services for payment processing. We do not store or collect your payment card details. That information is provided directly to our third-party payment processors whose use of your personal information is governed by their Privacy Policy. These payment processors adhere to the standards set by brands like Visa, Mastercard, American Express and Discover. Their requirements help ensure the secure handling of payment information.

Data Protection

We take the security of the data that we collect very seriously and have implemented industry standard technical and administrative measures to protect the personal data stored in our systems.
