CHALLENGES

Whether you are a weekend warrior or an armchair quarterback, get ready to try and make a 30-yard field goal or hit a target 20 yards away with that perfect spiral. The opportunities will be multiple as you try and achieve some gridiron glory in punt, pass and kick challenges.

Get your challenge-day card online and add it to your digital wallet now!



TOURNAMENTS

Our half width 100-yard long football field, complete with regulation NFL goal posts, will host a number of contests for everyone. Participate to see who can make the most consecutive field goals, throw the furthest distance, or become an accuracy passing champion. A wide variety of football tournaments for all ages will offer the chance to show off your prowess.
